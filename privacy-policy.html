Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

Introduction

100 Days 100 Books ("we," "our," or "us") operates the 100 Days 100 Books mobile application (the "Service") for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have when you use our Service.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information Stored on Your Device

Most of your app data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. This may include:

  • Reading progress: completed books, current challenge day, and reading position within a book
  • Saved insights: book insights you choose to save
  • App preferences: theme (light/dark/system), language, and onboarding answers
  • Anonymous user ID: a randomly generated identifier created on first launch to display in your profile (not linked to your name or email)
  • Subscription status cache: locally cached entitlement state after in-app purchases (when subscriptions are active)

Information We Do Not Collect Directly

  • We do not require account registration, email, or password to use the core reading experience
  • We do not collect your payment card details — purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • We do not upload your saved insights or reading history to our own backend at this time

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, certain third-party SDKs may automatically collect technical and usage information, such as:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • General app usage and session data (e.g. app opens, screens viewed)
  • Advertising or device identifiers, where permitted by your device settings and applicable law
  • Crash and performance diagnostics (if enabled by third-party tools)

How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: deliver daily book content, track your 100-day challenge progress, and restore your preferences
  • To manage subscriptions: verify trial and paid access through Apple or Google billing systems
  • To improve the app: understand usage patterns and fix issues through analytics tools
  • To comply with legal obligations: where required by applicable law

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Your progress, saved insights, and settings are stored on your device using secure local storage mechanisms provided by the operating system
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device

Security

  • We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Service
  • No method of electronic storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security

Data Retention

  • Local app data is retained on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app
  • Analytics data retained by third-party providers is governed by their respective policies

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process information:

Google / Firebase

  • Firebase Analytics: helps us understand how the app is used (e.g. sessions, feature usage)
  • Privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy

AppsFlyer

  • Mobile attribution and analytics platform (may collect device and campaign-related data when enabled)
  • Privacy policy: AppsFlyer Privacy Policy

Superwall

  • Subscription paywall and purchase flow management
  • Privacy policy: Superwall Privacy Policy

Apple App Store / Google Play

  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The Service may offer subscription plans (e.g. weekly access with a free trial, or a one-time lifetime purchase). Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not store your credit card or bank account information.

Subscription terms, including trial period, renewal, and cancellation, are shown in the app and in the respective store listing before purchase.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you (primarily your locally stored data visible in the app)
  • Delete locally stored data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of analytics: on iOS, use "Ask App Not to Track" and limit ad tracking in device settings; on Android, reset or limit your advertising ID in Google settings
  • Manage subscriptions: cancel or modify subscriptions through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings

To request assistance with privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact us using the email below.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Third-party service providers (such as Google, AppsFlyer, and Superwall) may process data on servers located outside your country. By using the Service, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.
