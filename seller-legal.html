3. Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

The App offers optional paid access to the full 100-day reading challenge. Book 1 is available free without a subscription. Payments are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store; we do not collect or store your payment card details.

3.1 Weekly subscription

  • Free trial: 3 days (where offered in your region).
  • After trial: recurring weekly subscription (example list price: USD $4.99/week — actual price shown in the App before you confirm).
  • Auto-renewal: Renews automatically unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Cancel: Google Play → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → 100 Days 100 Books → Cancel. On iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

3.2 Lifetime purchase (“All 100 Books”)

  • Free trial: 3 days (where offered), then one-time charge.
  • Price: one-time purchase (example list price: USD $34.99 — actual price shown in the App).
  • No renewal: This is not a subscription; it does not auto-renew.

3.3 Restore purchases

If you reinstall the App or change devices, use Restore purchases in the App profile. Restoration uses the same Google Play or Apple ID used for the original purchase.
